Below is a table that shows the franchise locations of Top Pot Donuts in the first column. The number of donuts to make are our numbers next to those locations and are based on the hours of operation - 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM - and a few factors unique to each location:

  • The mimimum number of customers per hour.
  • The maximum number of customers per hour.
  • The average number of doughnuts purchased per customer.

If you would like to change the store hours, please enter the time you want the franchises to open and close below and then click the "Change Time" button. If you would like to add a location to the table, please enter the required data in the Add Top Pot Donut Location form below and hit the "Add Location" button.
